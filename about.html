01. INTRODUCTION

What is ILUZTAR?

ILUZTAR (イルズター) is a creative studio focused on anime illustration, graphic branding, and photo manipulation. More than just a service provider, ILUZTAR also serves as a collaborative partner for artists who need help completing their work.

"Shine Through Art" — ILUZTAR's philosophy meaning "shine through art". This reflects two things:

  • Personal shine — Shining through our own work with strong visual identity
  • Collaborative shine — Helping other artists shine too ("We got you")

Main Mission

  1. Creating Original Art — Producing artwork with distinctive and characteristic visual identity
  2. Supporting Fellow Artists — Being a support system for artists experiencing artblock or overwhelmed with workload

Apa itu ILUZTAR?

ILUZTAR (イルズター) adalah studio kreatif yang berfokus pada anime illustration, graphic branding, dan photo manipulation. Lebih dari sekedar penyedia jasa, ILUZTAR juga menjadi collaborative partner untuk artist yang membutuhkan bantuan dalam menyelesaikan karya mereka.

02. VISUAL IDENTITY

ILUZTAR's Work Characteristics

ILUZTAR's work has distinctive features that are easily recognizable:

1. Heavy Textures

  • Use textures to add depth and character
  • Not just smooth and "clean", but has "soul"
  • Complex texture layers yet controlled

2. Rough Artistic Touch

  • Brush strokes are visible, not hidden
  • Intentional imperfections for character
  • Balance between precision and organic feel

3. Balanced Color Palette

  • Warm and cool tones that complement each other
  • Controlled saturation — not overly vibrant
  • Strategic use of accent colors for focal points

4. Narrative Composition

  • Every element tells a story
  • Dynamic poses and expressions
  • Atmosphere and mood as core elements

"Art that has soul — not perfect, but alive. Textures that breathe, colors that speak, compositions that move."

04. WORKFLOW

Standard Commission Process

1. Initial Contact & Brief

  • Client contacts via email or social media
  • Discussion about project requirements
  • ILUZTAR provides timeline and pricing estimates

2. Agreement & Down Payment

  • Terms & conditions agreed upon
  • 50% down payment to start project
  • Timeline and deliverables confirmed

3. Creation Process

  • Sketch / concept phase with approval
  • Lineart refinement
  • Coloring & shading
  • Final polish & effects
  • WIP updates at each important milestone

4. Revision & Finalization

  • 2-3 minor revisions included
  • Final approval from client
  • Remaining 50% payment

5. Delivery

  • High-resolution final file
  • Delivery via email or file sharing service

Collaboration Process (For Artists)

1. Artist reaches out with their project

  • Explain where they need help (sketch / line / color / bg / polish)
  • Share reference and existing WIP

2. Discussion & Scope Definition

  • Define exactly what ILUZTAR will contribute
  • Clear boundaries to protect artist's vision
  • Pricing based on scope of contribution

3. Collaborative Execution

  • Intense communication throughout
  • WIP sharing at each checkpoint
  • Artist has final say on everything

4. Handover

  • Completed contribution delivered
  • Artist retains full ownership
  • ILUZTAR may request portfolio credit (optional)

08. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: How long does a commission take?

A: Timeline varies by complexity. Simple illustrations typically 3-7 days, complex scenes 1-3 weeks. This will be clearly communicated before starting.

Q: Can I get revisions?

A: Yes! 2-3 minor revisions are included in the package. Major revisions (large concept changes after approval) may incur additional fees. Good communication at the start minimizes the need for major revisions.

Q: What about commercial use?

A: Commercial use rights need to be discussed and agreed upon upfront. Fees for commercial rights are higher than personal use. A clear contract will be created to define usage scope.

Q: Are source files provided?

A: High-resolution final images are always provided. Source files (PSD/CSP with layers) can be provided for an additional fee, depending on agreement and project type.

Q: How does collaboration work for artists?

A: The artist explains at which stage they need help (sketch, line, color, background, polish). ILUZTAR joins at that stage. The artist retains full creative control and ownership of the final work.

Q: Does ILUZTAR use AI?

A: No. ILUZTAR is a human artist who has mastered various manual digital techniques. All works are done by hand. No AI generation.

Q: Berapa lama pengerjaan commission?

A: Timeline bervariasi tergantung kompleksitas. Ilustrasi sederhana biasanya 3-7 hari, scene kompleks 1-3 minggu. Ini akan dikomunikasikan dengan jelas sebelum memulai.

Q: Apakah ada revisi?

A: Ya! 2-3 minor revisions included dalam package. Major revisions (perubahan concept besar setelah approval) akan dikenakan additional fee. Communication yang baik di tahap awal bisa minimize kebutuhan major revisions.

Q: Bagaimana dengan commercial use?

A: Commercial use rights perlu didiskusikan dan disetujui di awal. Fee untuk commercial rights lebih tinggi dari personal use. Clear contract akan dibuat untuk mengatur usage scope.

Q: Apakah source files disediakan?

A: High-resolution final image selalu disediakan. Source files (PSD/CSP dengan layers) bisa disediakan dengan additional fee, depending on agreement dan project type.

Q: Bagaimana collaboration process untuk artist?

A: Artist menjelaskan di tahap mana mereka butuh bantuan (sketch, line, color, background, polish). ILUZTAR akan join di tahap tersebut. Artist tetap punya full creative control dan ownership atas final work.

Q: Apakah ILUZTAR menggunakan AI?

A: Tidak. ILUZTAR adalah human artist yang menguasai berbagai teknik digital manual. Semua karya dikerjakan by hand. No AI generation.
