Scenario 3 - Specialization Split:

Artist jago character tapi butuh background kompleks → Artist fokus character → ILUZTAR handle background → Hasil: karya yang balance

10. COPYRIGHT & CREDIT POLICY
10. COPYRIGHT & CREDIT POLICY

Prinsip Utama:

RESPECT & TRANSPARENCY

Ownership Karya:

1. Solo Work (Full ILUZTAR):

  • ILUZTAR owns the artwork
  • Client gets usage rights sesuai agreement
  • Credit: "Art by ILUZTAR"

2. Collaboration (Artist + ILUZTAR):

  • Lead Artist retains ownership
  • ILUZTAR adalah contributor, bukan co-owner
  • Credit format: "Art by [Artist Name], [specific contribution] by ILUZTAR"
  • Contoh: "Art by @ArtistX, Background by ILUZTAR"
  • ILUZTAR TIDAK boleh claim sebagai full artwork sendiri

3. Commission for Client:

  • Client owns the commissioned artwork
  • ILUZTAR retains portfolio rights (unless NDA)
  • Credit: "Commissioned by [Client], Art by ILUZTAR"

ILUZTAR NEVER:

  • Claim collaborative work as 100% own creation
  • Use artist's work in portfolio without permission & credit
  • Sell/redistribute collaborative work without agreement
  • Remove or hide original artist's credit

Portfolio & Social Media:

  • Collaborative work akan di-post dengan CLEAR CREDIT
  • Tag original artist kalau memungkinkan
  • Jika artist request tidak dipublish → ILUZTAR respects that

Filosofi: "Your vision, your ownership. ILUZTAR is here to help you shine, not to steal your light."

12. FAQ - FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Apakah ILUZTAR hanya untuk artist profesional?

A: Tidak! ILUZTAR terbuka untuk artist di semua level - dari yang baru mulai komisi hingga yang sudah established. Yang penting adalah mutual respect dan komunikasi yang jelas.

Q: Bagaimana kalau style saya sangat berbeda dengan ILUZTAR?

A: ILUZTAR trained untuk adaptif dengan berbagai style. Bisa provide references dan guide, dan ILUZTAR akan adjust workflow untuk match dengan vision kamu.

Q: Apakah ILUZTAR bisa kerja dengan deadline ketat?

A: Tergantung workload saat itu. Untuk rush orders, ada additional fee. Best practice: kontak ILUZTAR sedini mungkin untuk ensure availability.

Q: Bagaimana dengan NDA dan confidentiality?

A: ILUZTAR respects NDA sepenuhnya. Untuk project confidential, karya tidak akan di-share di portfolio atau social media tanpa explicit permission.

Q: Apakah bisa trial collaboration dulu sebelum commit ke project besar?

A: Absolutely! ILUZTAR encourage untuk mulai dengan project kecil dulu untuk test compatibility dan workflow.

Q: Bagaimana kalau saya tidak puas dengan hasilnya?

A: Communication is key. ILUZTAR provide WIP updates dan checkpoints untuk ensure kamu happy dengan direction. 2-3 minor revisions included. Kalau masih ada issue, mari diskusi untuk find solution.

Q: Apakah ILUZTAR menggunakan AI?

A: ILUZTAR adalah human artist yang menguasai berbagai teknik digital manual. Semua karya dikerjakan by hand menggunakan software seperti Photoshop, Clip Studio Paint, dan tools profesional lainnya. No AI generation.

Q: Is ILUZTAR only for professional artists?

A: No! ILUZTAR is open to artists at all levels - from those just starting commissions to established ones. What's important is mutual respect and clear communication.

Q: What if my style is very different from ILUZTAR?

A: ILUZTAR is trained to be adaptive to various styles. You can provide references and guidance, and ILUZTAR will adjust workflow to match your vision.

Q: Can ILUZTAR work with tight deadlines?

A: Depends on the current workload. For rush orders, there's an additional fee. Best practice: contact ILUZTAR as early as possible to ensure availability.

Q: What about NDA and confidentiality?

A: ILUZTAR fully respects NDAs. For confidential projects, work will not be shared in portfolio or social media without explicit permission.

Q: Can we do a trial collaboration first before committing to a large project?

A: Absolutely! ILUZTAR encourages starting with a small project first to test compatibility and workflow.

Q: What if I'm not satisfied with the result?

A: Communication is key. ILUZTAR provides WIP updates and checkpoints to ensure you're happy with the direction. 2-3 minor revisions included. If there are still issues, let's discuss to find a solution.

Q: Does ILUZTAR use AI?

A: ILUZTAR is a human artist who masters various manual digital techniques. All work is done by hand using software like Photoshop, Clip Studio Paint, and other professional tools. No AI generation.
